OEM Contract Workflow

1
CAD Blueprint Engineering & FEA Simulation
Clients supply technical mechanical blueprints (STEP, IGES, or DXF). Our design engineers verify fitment clearances and execute Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to confirm structure stress resilience under high physical load thresholds before cutting raw steel/aluminum.
2
CNC Toolpath Generation & Rapid Prototyping
We translate approved structural CAD mockups into precise multi-axis CNC machine toolpaths. A rapid physical prototype is milled within 48 hours for mechanical test checking and engineering sign-off.
3
ISO 9001:2015 Automated Production
Upon signed approval of the prototype, high-volume automated production cycles initiate. Material batches carry mill certifications to guarantee metal chemistry and structural load durability profiles.
4
CMM Inspection & Aerospace Anodization
Every production batch undergoes Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) dimensional checks relative to blueprint margins. Components then receive aerospace-grade powder coating, hard-coat anodizing, or custom laser engravings before secure packing.
