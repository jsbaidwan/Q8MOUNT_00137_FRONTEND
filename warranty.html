Warranty Information

Manufacturer's Warranty

Most products sold by Q8mount come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period and coverage vary by product and manufacturer.

Warranty Coverage

Typical warranty coverage includes:

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Malfunctions under normal use
  • Parts and labor (varies by manufacturer)

Not Covered

Warranties typically do NOT cover:

  • Accidental damage or misuse
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Cosmetic damage
