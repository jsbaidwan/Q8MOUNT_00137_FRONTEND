The P605-5083 Weld Racing Cap series is engineered specifically for performance drag and highway wheels. This mount provides an exceptionally secure fit that keeps dust and debris out of the wheel hub bearing, assuring long life under extreme conditions. The push-through layout delivers tool-less, fast installation directly from the rear of the wheel center bore.
