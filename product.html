The P605-5083 Weld Racing Cap series is engineered specifically for performance drag and highway wheels. This mount provides an exceptionally secure fit that keeps dust and debris out of the wheel hub bearing, assuring long life under extreme conditions. The push-through layout delivers tool-less, fast installation directly from the rear of the wheel center bore.

Installation Instructions:

  1. Jack up the vehicle securely and remove the wheel assembly.
  2. Clean the center bore of the wheel thoroughly using a wire brush to remove rust or debris.
  3. Insert the new Weld Racing center cap from the rear side of the wheel center bore.
  4. Push firmly until the cap's flange is completely seated against the wheel's rear mounting pad.
  5. Re-install the wheel assembly onto the vehicle and torque the lug nuts to factory specifications.
